How It Works

My Design Process

A clear, no-surprises workflow from the first message to the final handoff.

01
Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute call to talk through your goals, your audience, your brand, and what you want your site to achieve. I ask a lot of questions — the better I understand your business, the better the outcome.

02
Proposal & Timeline

After the call I send a detailed proposal covering scope, deliverables, price, and a realistic timeline. No vague estimates — you'll know exactly what you're getting and when, before any work starts.

03
Design & Build

I design every page in Figma first so you can see and approve the look before a single line of code is written. Once you're happy with the design, I build the site — clean, fast, and fully responsive across all devices.

04
Review & Launch

You get a staging link to review the full site before it goes live. We do one round of revisions, then I handle the launch — domain connection, SSL, speed checks, and a final cross-device test. Your site goes live ready to impress.
